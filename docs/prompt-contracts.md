# Prompt Contracts

## Research Brain

Purpose:

- turn direct-mode intake into compact sales research

Inputs:

- product
- offer summary
- ICP
- company name
- company website
- contact title
- operator notes

Outputs:

- company summary
- persona summary
- pains
- hooks
- buying signals
- source notes
- confidence

Rules:

- stay concise
- optimize for cold-call utility
- avoid unsupported claims

## Strategy Brain

Purpose:

- convert research into a compact `call_brief`

Inputs:

- product
- research packet

Outputs:

- summary
- value props
- proof points
- opening lines
- qualification questions
- objection tree
- CTAs
- risk flags
- forbidden claims

Rules:

- keep the brief realtime-safe
- produce short usable openings
- bias toward questions, not monologues

## Realtime Call Brain

Purpose:

- execute the live conversation

Prompt layers:

1. global behavior
2. sales methodology
3. campaign strategy
4. lead brief
5. live state

Rules:

- be concise
- ask one question at a time
- avoid unsupported facts
- use approved claims only
- prefer micro-commitments
- tolerate interruption

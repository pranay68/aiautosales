# Product Requirements

## Goal

Build an autonomous outbound sales platform that can:

- ingest a product and target profile
- research a company/contact
- generate a tailored call brief
- execute a realtime AI-led sales call
- store transcript, outcome, and follow-up

## Primary Users

- founder-led sales teams
- SDR/BDR teams
- RevOps operators
- agencies running outbound campaigns

## Modes

### Direct Mode

User provides:

- product
- company
- phone number
- optional contact
- optional notes

System returns:

- research packet
- call brief
- policy decision
- call session
- transcript
- follow-up

### Campaign Mode

User provides:

- product
- ICP
- campaign objective
- lead list or lead source

System returns:

- prioritized prospects
- generated briefs
- scheduled calls
- outcome reporting

## MVP Scope

- direct mode intake
- research generation
- strategy generation
- policy gate
- outbound call session creation
- voice session initialization
- transcript logging
- post-call follow-up creation
- call evaluation scorecard

## Non-MVP

- fully autonomous web-scale prospect discovery
- multilingual sales execution
- advanced CRM syncs
- model fine-tuning
- late-stage complex deal closing workflows

## Success Metrics

- direct lead to call brief completion rate
- direct lead to call session completion rate
- median research generation time
- median call setup time
- first response latency in call
- follow-up generation rate
- operator review burden
